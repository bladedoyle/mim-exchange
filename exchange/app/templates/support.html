FAQ

What happens if I send more (or less) than the amount I requested?

The exchange will be adjusted to match the amount recieved and executed at the current market rate

What happens if I send multiple transactions for the same exchange?

Each exchange will accept only 1 transaction. Any additional transactions will be returned to the refund address you provide

How do I provide a refund address?

If an exchange fails to execute for any reason, or if multiple transcations are received for a signle exchange you will be prompted to provide a refund address. If you have already exited the exchange workflow you can "Track" the exchange using the "Exchange ID" to re-join the workflow and get the refund address prompt

How are exchange rates calculated?

Quoted exchange rates are valid for {{ cfg.exchange_rate_expire }} minutes after which the rate will be recalculated based on the market prices when the transaction is received

What fees are charged by MIME?

A {{ cfg.exchange_fee }}% fee is charged on all exchanges
Transaction fees are paid by the user

Why did I receive slightly more or less than the quoted exchange?

One or both of the following may apply:
Network transaction fees are paid by the user out of the amount sent
Your sent transaction was confirmed after the quoted exchange rate expired and was recalculated at the new market rate

What happens if my transaction takes hours (or days) to be confirmed?

Exchanges are vaild for {{ cfg.incomplete_swap_expire }} days and will be executed at the current market rate when the send transaction is confirmed on the blockchain

What can I do if I have not received my coins?

Contact MIM Exchange support at {{ cfg.support_email }} and provide the Exchange ID and the Send Transaction ID

What happens if I lose my Exchange ID?

The Excahnge ID allows you to track the status of your exchange. If you lose the ID the exchange will execute but you will not be able to look up the details
